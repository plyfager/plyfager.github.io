I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at [Singapore Management University (SMU)](https://www.smu.edu.sg/) under the guidance of Professor [He Shengfeng](http://www.shengfenghe.com/). My research interests include **Motion Synthesis**, **World Models**, and **Generation Models**.

Prior to my Ph.D., I worked as a Research Engineer at the [Shanghai AI Lab](https://www.shlab.org.cn/)'s [OpenMMLab](https://openmmlab.com/) team (2021 - 2023), where I led the development of [MMGeneration](https://github.com/open-mmlab/mmgeneration) and participated in [MMagic](https://github.com/open-mmlab/mmagic), contributing over 60k lines of code to the OpenMMLab ecosystem.

I obtained my Master's degree in Mathematics from [Shandong University (SDU)](https://www.sdu.edu.cn/) and my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from [East China Normal University (ECNU)](https://www.ecnu.edu.cn/).

## Research Interests

- **Motion Synthesis**: Exploring long-term motion generation, terrain-aware interactive motion, and physically plausible motion.
- **World Models**: Investigating world models for interactive motion generation.
- **Generation Models**: Diffusion models, GANs, and their applications in vision tasks.
